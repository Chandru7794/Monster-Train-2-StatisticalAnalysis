---
name: Always log after interpretation
description: Never ask the user if they want to log — just log it after they've shared observations
type: feedback
---

After the user shares observations and interpretation, always log it to the journal immediately. Do not ask "want to log this?" — just do it.

**Why:** User has explicitly said to stop asking. Once they've explained something, logging is assumed.

**How to apply:** After any user observation/interpretation in an EDA session, log it and tell them what was logged. Skip the confirmation question entirely.
